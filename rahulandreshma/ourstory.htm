March 2014 - Reshma and Rahul embarked on an adventure that would change their lives forever without even realizing it. They both moved to New York City within a week of each other to pursue their dreams of living in the big city.

February 2015 - Reshma and Rahul were looking for love the old fashion way, through an online dating site. Rahul sent a message to Reshma asking her thoughts on Serial, a podcast that was popular at the time and she responded. The two of them hit it off and began texting for a few weeks until Rahul finally convinced Reshma to meet in person. Reshma was reluctant as she spent the weekend before celebrating her sister's birthday and wasn't in her best form. But Rahul insisted they meet that night so she went reluctantly to a very interesting first date. The first 30 minutes was silent and extremely awkward as they walked in the cold from bar to bar to try and find somewhere to sit. They finally found a spot and spent the entire night talking until the bar finally kicked them out. Over the coming months, they spent hours talking about life, music, dating, etc and grew closer and closer without even knowing what was happening. The rest was history...

November 2017 - After dating for over two and a half years, and knowing they were lucky and meant to be, Rahul decided to put a ring on it. He had been working really hard at his job and finally got the promotion he'd been waiting for. He was so elated that he went to his sister's house to pick up the ring he had sent there, so he could propose later that week. On the train home, an anxiety riddled Rahul realized that he couldn't wait any longer and he had to ask ASAP.

Reshma rushed home to plan a few surprises to celebrate his big promotion, but little did she know he had a surprise as well.
